Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 12, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Burn Journal collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app and website.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: We do not require you to provide personal information to use Burn Journal. No account or email is needed.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience. This data does not identify you.
  • Device Data: We may collect device type, OS version, and crash logs for debugging and support.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve Burn Journal’s features and user experience
  • To analyze usage trends and fix bugs
  • To ensure the security and integrity of your data

Data Storage & Security

All your journal entries are stored locally on your device. We do not store or transmit your private entries to our servers. We use industry-standard security practices to protect any data we do collect.

Third-Party Services

Burn Journal may use trusted third-party services (such as analytics or crash reporting) that comply with strict privacy standards. These services do not access your private journal entries.

Children’s Privacy

Burn Journal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights & Choices

  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all data from your device.
  • For questions or requests, contact us at support@burnjournal.app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
